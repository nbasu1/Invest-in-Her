What is Invest in Her?

Invest in Her is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit dedicated to building the next generation of female leaders in economics and finance. With only two female Nobel laureates, economics is infamous for its large gender gap. Women across the country fall behind men in terms of financial literacy, and consequently, women struggle to be economically independent. As high school students, we aspire to solve these fundamental issues at their root by educating middle school and high school girls in economics. We hope to inspire young women to take control of the money in their lives and even to pursue careers in economics.
